Ultrasound–Diabetes Multimodal Prediction

University of Michigan, Graduate Researcher (PI: Prof. Liyue Shen), 2025.03.

密歇根大学，研究生研究员（导师：Liyue Shen 教授），2025.03。

Built a unified pipeline linking clinical variables (e.g., BMI, age, labs) with skeletal-muscle ultrasound (deltoid, vastus; SAX/LAX views) for diabetes and insulin-resistance prediction. Trained clinical-only baselines and developed segmentation models (U-Net/MedSAM) to derive quantitative muscle features (echogenicity/density, thickness, cross-sectional area), with radiomics/texture descriptors and patient-level cross-validated evaluation. Designed and compared early- and late-fusion multimodal strategies to predict insulin resistance / type 2 diabetes.

构建了将临床变量（如 BMI、年龄、化验指标）与骨骼肌超声（三角肌、股四头肌；短轴/长轴切面）相结合的统一流程，用于糖尿病与胰岛素抵抗预测。训练了仅基于临床数据的基线模型，并开发了基于 U-Net/MedSAM 的分割方法以提取定量肌肉特征（回声强度/密度、厚度、横截面积），结合影像组学/纹理特征与患者级别的交叉验证评测。设计并比较了早融合与晚融合等多模态融合策略，用于预测胰岛素抵抗/二型糖尿病。

Biomedical Pathology RAG Benchmark (DIAG)

University of Michigan, Multidisciplinary Design Program (MDP), Team Lead, 2025.01.

密歇根大学，跨学科设计项目（MDP），团队负责人，2025.01。

Lead a subteam designing a pathology-specific retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) benchmark for biomedical and pathology LLMs, integrating BioLLM and DeepSeek models with modular retrievers and embeddings. Developed standardized datasets and retrieval/generation evaluation metrics (e.g., Recall@K, nDCG, faithfulness) together with evidence-citation pipelines for benchmarking.

在跨学科设计项目（MDP）框架下领导子团队，设计面向生物医学与病理学大语言模型的检索增强生成（RAG）评测基准，集成 BioLLM 与 DeepSeek 模型及模块化检索器与向量表示。构建了标准化数据集，以及检索与生成评测指标（如 Recall@K、nDCG、忠实度）与证据引用流程。

Genetic Disease Research Project in Data Science

BNBU, Project Subteam Leader, 2022.10 – 2023.04.

北京师范大学-香港浸会大学联合国际学院（BNBU），项目小组负责人，2022.10 – 2023.04。

Led data analysis and modeling using Decision Trees, Gradient Boosting Trees, Random Forests, and Logistic Regression; the final Logistic Regression model achieved 93% accuracy. Results were published at IEEE MedAI 2023 and implemented in local hospitals.

主导数据分析与建模，训练决策树、梯度提升树、随机森林与逻辑回归模型；在发现召回率欠佳后应用过拟合缓解技术提升性能，最终逻辑回归模型达到 93% 的准确率。相关成果发表于 IEEE MedAI 2023，并已在当地医院落地应用。
