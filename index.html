Guli Zhu

M.S. Student in Health Data Science, University of Michigan — Ann Arbor

I am a Master of Health Data Science student at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (2024–2026), and a graduate researcher in Prof. Liyue Shen's lab, where I work at the intersection of medical AI, multimodal vision-language models (VLMs), and model editing. My current research designs clinically grounded benchmarks for evaluating how reliably, locally, and generalizably knowledge-editing methods can correct post-deployment errors in medical VLMs.

Previously, I earned a B.S. in Data Science from Beijing Normal-Hong Kong Baptist University (BNBU) and interned as a machine learning engineer at ByteDance, working on multimodal recommendation systems. My broader interests span medical visual question answering (Med-VQA), trustworthy and reliable AI, and multimodal biomedical data science.
