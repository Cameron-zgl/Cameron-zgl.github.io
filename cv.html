★ Model Editing for Medical Multimodal LM — BioMed-AI Lab, University of Michigan
Graduate Researcher (PI: Prof. Liyue Shen)
2025.06 – Present · Ann Arbor, MI
  • Formulated localized factual editing for medical VLMs and designed a six-dimension framework centered on locality vs. generality, modality shift, compositional edits, and temporal stability.
  • Built a fully reproducible evaluation stack (pre/post-edit scoring, automatic grading, evidence attribution, and audit sampling), enabling ablation-driven analysis rather than anecdotal case studies.
  • Curated Med-VQA–centric testbeds (VQA-RAD, PathVQA, PMC-VQA, SLAKE) and temporal CT subsets; created stress tests with text/vision perturbations, cross-modality locality probes (edit on CT; probe on CXR/MRI/US), and rare-disease tails.
  • Benchmarked multiple editing approaches across HuatuoGPT-Vision-34B, LLaVA-Med-7B, and related baselines on a 6-dimension benchmark; preparing a public release of the specification and evaluation code. (Accepted ECCV 2026)
  • 提出面向医疗 VLM 的局部化事实编辑方法，设计了围绕局部性（Locality）与泛化性（Generality）、模态迁移、组合式编辑及时间稳定性的六维评测框架。
  • 构建了完全可复现的评测体系（编辑前后打分、自动评分、证据归因与抽样审查），支持基于消融实验的系统性分析，而非个案式验证。
  • 整理了以医疗视觉问答（Med-VQA）为核心的测试集（VQA-RAD、PathVQA、PMC-VQA、SLAKE）及时序 CT 子集；构建了文本/视觉扰动压力测试、跨模态局部性探测（在 CT 上编辑、在 CXR/MRI/超声上探测）以及罕见病长尾测试。
  • 在该六维基准上对 HuatuoGPT-Vision-34B、LLaVA-Med-7B 及相关基线模型评测了多种编辑方法；正在筹备评测规范与评测代码的公开发布。（已被 ECCV 2026 接收）
★ Ultrasound–Diabetes Multimodal Prediction — University of Michigan
Graduate Researcher (PI: Prof. Liyue Shen)
2025.03 – Present · Ann Arbor, MI
  • Built a unified pipeline linking clinical variables (e.g., BMI, age, labs) with skeletal-muscle ultrasound (deltoid, vastus; SAX/LAX) for diabetes/insulin-resistance prediction.
  • Trained clinical-only baselines and developed segmentation (U-Net/MedSAM) to derive quantitative muscle features (echogenicity/density, thickness, cross-sectional area).
  • Engineered feature extraction and QC for masks; computed radiomics/texture descriptors; standardized patient-level splits with cross-validated evaluation.
  • Designed and compared multimodal fusion strategies (early/late fusion) to predict insulin resistance/T2D; documented metrics and ablation findings.
  • 构建了将临床变量（如 BMI、年龄、化验指标）与骨骼肌超声（三角肌、股四头肌；短轴/长轴切面）相结合的统一预测流程，用于糖尿病/胰岛素抵抗预测。
  • 训练了仅基于临床数据的基线模型，并开发了基于 U-Net/MedSAM 的分割方法，用以提取肌肉的定量特征（回声强度/密度、厚度、横截面积）。
  • 设计了分割掩膜的特征提取与质量控制流程；计算了影像组学/纹理特征；建立了以患者为单位的标准化数据划分及交叉验证评测。
  • 设计并比较了早融合/晚融合等多模态融合策略以预测胰岛素抵抗/二型糖尿病；系统记录了评测指标与消融实验结果。
